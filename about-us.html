Company Profile

Introduction

“Expressway Shipping” is a leading Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) in the Indian subcontinent, with a strong presence in the Far East, Middle East, and Upper Gulf regions.

At Expressway Shipping, we specialize in providing NVOCC services to facilitate seamless cargo movement across maritime logistics networks in India, the Gulf, Far East, Southeast Asia, and Europe. Our expertise lies in managing full container loads, special equipment, and project cargo through our extensive network of ports and Inland Container Depots (ICDs), which is one of our core strengths.

Our team consists of highly skilled, experienced, and qualified professionals who execute daily logistics operations with precision. This combination of expertise has positioned us as a premier NVOCC company within the shipping and logistics industry.

We have extensive experience in handling shipments, including:

  • Exports from India to the Far East, Gulf, Southeast Asia, and Europe
  • Imports from China, the Far East, and Europe to India

As we continue to align with emerging trends in the shipping support services industry, our company has grown significantly, offering a diverse range of services to importers, exporters, ship owners, and principals.

Customer satisfaction remains our guiding principle, as it has been proven consistently over the years and will continue to be our focus."
