AGENCY

Ship Agency Services

Our Ship Agency division, established in 2007, has grown into a trusted and respected player in the shipping industry. Starting with our first office in Kolkata, we have expanded our presence across India and have established international representation at Port Klang. Today, we proudly serve a broad range of clients and have earned a significant market share, becoming a go-to partner for shipping operations.

Strategic Presence Across India

Our expansive network of offices strategically located in major ports across India ensures that we can offer comprehensive and responsive ship agency services wherever you need them. Whether it's the bustling ports of Mumbai, Chennai, or smaller yet critical locations, our team is always on the ground, ready to manage your vessel’s requirements with efficiency and expertise.

International Representation

Recognizing the importance of global connectivity, we have extended our reach beyond India with an international presence at Port Klang, one of Southeast Asia’s most vital maritime hubs. This strategic location allows us to facilitate smooth operations for vessels entering or leaving the region, providing our clients with seamless international shipping support.

Expertise and Recognition in the Shipping Fraternity

Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability and professionalism in the shipping fraternity. Our deep industry knowledge, coupled with our unwavering commitment to excellence, has earned us recognition and respect among ship owners, charterers, and operators. Our market share continues to grow as more clients trust us to manage their vessel operations with precision and care.

Comprehensive Ship Agency Services

Our ship agency services cover every aspect of port call management, ensuring that your vessel's needs are met efficiently and effectively. Our services include: 

  • Port Clearance and Documentation : We handle all necessary paperwork and regulatory compliance to ensure that your vessel can enter and exit ports without delays. Our familiarity with local customs and port regulations allows us to navigate the complexities of port clearance smoothly.
  • Crew Management : We manage crew changes, medical assistance, and other crew-related services to ensure the well-being and efficiency of your vessel’s staff during port calls.
  • Provisions and Supplies : We coordinate the timely delivery of provisions, fuel, and other essential supplies, ensuring that your vessel is fully stocked and ready for its next journey.
  • Cargo Operations Support : Our team provides comprehensive support for loading and unloading operations, working closely with port authorities and terminal operators to ensure that cargo handling is carried out efficiently and safely.
  • Emergency Response : In the event of unforeseen circumstances, our agency team is on standby to provide immediate assistance, minimizing downtime and ensuring that any issues are resolved promptly.

Commitment to Excellence

Our success in the ship agency business is driven by our commitment to providing top-tier service with a focus on reliability, transparency, and client satisfaction. We understand the critical nature of shipping operations, and our goal is to be a dependable partner that you can trust to manage your vessel’s needs with the highest level of professionalism.

By choosing our Ship Agency services, you are partnering with a team that has a proven track record of excellence. Whether your vessel is docking at one of India’s many ports or at our international location in Port Klang, you can count on us to deliver exceptional service, every time.
