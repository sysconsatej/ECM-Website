About Expressway Shipping

"Expressway Shipping" is a leading Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) in the Indian subcontinent, with a strong presence in the Far East, Middle East, and Upper Gulf regions. At Expressway Shipping, we specialize in providing NVOCC services to facilitate seamless cargo movement across maritime logistics networks in India, the Gulf, Far East, Southeast Asia, and Europe. Our expertise lies in managing full container loads, special equipment, and project cargo through our extensive network of ports and Inland Container Depots (ICDs), which is one of our core strengths.

We, at Expressway Shipping provide Non-Vessel Operating Common (NVOCC) Services to our customers In leveraging cargo movements around/across the ‘maritime logistics domains in india, Gulf, Far East, South East Asia and Europe. Our expertise in the field of shipping full container loads, special equipment and project cargo using our extensive network of ports and ICDS, which being of our key forte.
